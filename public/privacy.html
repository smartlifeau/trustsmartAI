Section 01

Who we are

TrustSmart AI is a product of SmartLife AU, operated by SmartLife AU, an Australian sole trader business with ABN 26 103 403 893 and ASIC-registered business name. We are based in Australia and registered for GST.

This Privacy Policy explains how SmartLife AU collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information in connection with your use of TrustSmart AI (available at trustsmartai.com.au).

We are bound by the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) contained in that Act.

Section 02

What information we collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate TrustSmart AI. Depending on how you use the service, this may include:

  • Submitted content — text, URLs, images, or screenshots you submit for genuineness checking
  • Account information — name and email address if you create a free or Pro account via Clerk
  • Payment information — billing details for Pro subscribers, processed by Stripe
  • Usage data — anonymised analytics such as pages visited, input mode used, and general usage patterns
  • Technical data — browser type, device type, and approximate geographic region, collected automatically via analytics
  • Contact information — only if you contact us directly via email

Guest users (not signed in) may use up to 3 free checks per day without providing any personal information.

Section 03

Account information (Clerk)

TrustSmart AI uses Clerk for user authentication. Creating an account is optional for guest users but required to access more than 3 free checks per day or to subscribe to Pro.

When you create an account or sign in, Clerk collects and stores:

  • Your email address
  • Your first and last name (if provided)
  • Sign-in method (email or Google)
  • Session and authentication tokens
  • Sign-in history and device information

Clerk is a US-based authentication service. Your account data is stored on Clerk's servers in accordance with their privacy policy, available at clerk.com/legal/privacy. Clerk is compliant with SOC 2 Type II standards.

SmartLife AU receives your name and email address from Clerk when you are signed in, solely for the purpose of displaying your name in the app and associating your account with your Pro subscription.

Section 04

Payment information (Stripe)

TrustSmart AI uses Stripe to process Pro subscription payments. SmartLife AU does not collect, store, or have access to your credit card or banking details at any time.

When you subscribe to TrustSmart AI Pro, Stripe collects and stores:

  • Your payment card details (stored securely by Stripe, not by us)
  • Your billing email address
  • Your billing name and address
  • Transaction history and subscription status

Stripe is a US-based payment processor. Your payment data is handled in accordance with Stripe's privacy policy at stripe.com/au/privacy. Stripe is PCI DSS Level 1 certified.

SmartLife AU receives confirmation of successful payment from Stripe via webhook, which is used solely to activate your Pro subscription. We also receive your billing email to issue GST tax invoices.

GST tax invoices are issued automatically by Stripe on behalf of SmartLife AU for each billing cycle. These are sent to your billing email address and are available in your Stripe customer portal.

Section 05

How your submitted content is handled

Important: Content you submit (text, URLs, images, screenshots) is transmitted to Anthropic's API for AI analysis. It is not stored by SmartLife AU on any server or database after your check is complete.

When you submit content for a genuineness check:

  • Your content is sent from your browser to our secure serverless function hosted on Netlify
  • The serverless function forwards your content to the Anthropic Claude API for analysis
  • The analysis result is returned to your browser and displayed on screen
  • No copy of your submitted content or the result is retained by SmartLife AU after this process completes

Be mindful that content you submit may contain personal information about yourself or others. You should only submit content you have the right to share, and avoid submitting sensitive personal information unnecessarily.

Section 06

Image and screenshot handling

Images and screenshots are processed in memory only. They are not saved, logged, stored, or used for any purpose beyond the immediate analysis request you initiate.

When you upload an image or screenshot:

  • The file is converted to base64 format in your browser
  • It is transmitted securely (HTTPS) to our serverless function and then to the Anthropic API
  • Once the analysis response is returned, the image data is discarded
  • SmartLife AU cannot view or access images you have submitted

Screenshots may contain personal information about third parties. You are responsible for ensuring you have appropriate authority to submit any content you upload.

Section 07

Third-party services

TrustSmart AI uses the following third-party services. Each has their own privacy policy and data handling practices:

  • Anthropic Claude API — AI content analysis. Your submitted content is processed by Anthropic's systems. See anthropic.com/legal/privacy. Anthropic does not use API-submitted content to train their models by default.
  • Clerk — User authentication and account management. See clerk.com/legal/privacy.
  • Stripe — Payment processing and subscription management. See stripe.com/au/privacy.
  • Netlify — Hosting and serverless functions. Your requests pass through Netlify's infrastructure. See netlify.com/privacy.
  • Google Analytics (GA4) — Anonymised usage analytics. IP addresses are anonymised. You can opt out via Google's opt-out tools.
Section 08

How we use your information

We use the information we collect only for the following purposes:

  • To provide the genuineness checking service you have requested
  • To manage your account and authenticate your identity via Clerk
  • To process your Pro subscription payment via Stripe
  • To issue GST tax invoices for Pro subscriptions
  • To display your name in the app when you are signed in
  • To monitor and improve the performance and reliability of TrustSmart AI
  • To understand aggregate usage patterns and improve the product
  • To respond to enquiries you send us directly
  • To comply with our legal obligations under Australian law, including GST obligations

We do not use your submitted content to train AI models. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for marketing purposes.

Section 09

How we store and protect your data

Submitted content (text, URLs, images, screenshots) is not stored by SmartLife AU. It is processed transiently and discarded after each check.

Account data is stored by Clerk on their secure infrastructure. Payment data is stored by Stripe on their PCI DSS certified infrastructure. SmartLife AU does not maintain a separate database of user personal information.

Our infrastructure uses HTTPS encryption for all data in transit. Our serverless functions run in Netlify's secure environment and your Anthropic API key is never exposed to the browser.

Any emails you send to us are stored in our business email system and retained only as long as necessary to resolve your enquiry.

Section 10

Cookies and analytics

TrustSmart AI uses Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymised usage data. GA4 may set cookies in your browser to distinguish unique sessions.

Clerk also sets authentication cookies and tokens in your browser to maintain your signed-in session. These are essential for the app to function correctly when you are signed in.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or any third-party marketing trackers.

You can disable Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Section 11

Cross-border data transfers

Some of our third-party service providers are based in the United States. By using TrustSmart AI, you acknowledge that your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries outside Australia.

These transfers occur with the following providers:

  • Anthropic — submitted content is processed on US-based servers
  • Clerk — account data is stored on US-based servers
  • Stripe — payment data is processed on US and international servers
  • Netlify — hosting infrastructure is based in the US
  • Google Analytics — analytics data is processed by Google globally

We take reasonable steps to ensure these providers maintain appropriate data protection standards. Where applicable, these providers are bound by standard contractual clauses or equivalent protections.
