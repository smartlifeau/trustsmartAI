Section 01

Who we are

TrustSmart AI is a product of SmartLife AU, an Australian sole trader business with a registered ABN and ASIC business name. We are based in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia.

This Privacy Policy explains how SmartLife AU collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information in connection with your use of TrustSmart AI (available at trustsmartai.com.au or any subdomain thereof).

We are bound by the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) contained in that Act.

Section 02

What information we collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate TrustSmart AI. Depending on how you use the service, this may include:

  • Submitted content — text, URLs, images, or screenshots you submit for genuineness checking
  • Usage data — anonymised analytics such as pages visited, input mode used (text / screenshot / image / URL), and general usage patterns
  • Technical data — browser type, device type, and approximate geographic region (country/state level only), collected automatically via analytics
  • Contact information — only if you contact us directly via email (name and email address)

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect your name, email address, or any identifying information simply for using the app.

Section 03

How your submitted content is handled

Important: Content you submit (text, URLs, images, screenshots) is transmitted to Anthropic's API for AI analysis. It is not stored by SmartLife AU on any server or database after your check is complete.

When you submit content for a genuineness check, the following occurs:

  • Your content is sent from your browser to our secure serverless function hosted on Netlify
  • The serverless function forwards your content to the Anthropic Claude API for analysis
  • The analysis result is returned to your browser and displayed on screen
  • No copy of your submitted content or the result is retained by SmartLife AU after this process completes

Be mindful that content you submit — particularly in text or screenshot form — may contain personal information about yourself or others (for example, a WhatsApp message or social media post). You should only submit content you have the right to share, and avoid submitting sensitive personal information unnecessarily.

Section 04

Image and screenshot handling

Images and screenshots are processed in memory only. They are not saved, logged, stored, or used for any purpose beyond the immediate analysis request you initiate.

When you upload an image or screenshot:

  • The file is converted to a base64 encoded format in your browser
  • It is transmitted securely (HTTPS) to our serverless function and then to the Anthropic API
  • Once the analysis response is returned, the image data is discarded — it is not written to disk, stored in a database, or retained in any log
  • SmartLife AU cannot view or access images you have submitted

Screenshots may contain personal information about third parties (names, profile photos, private messages). You are responsible for ensuring you have appropriate authority to submit any content you upload. TrustSmart AI is not intended to be used to analyse content about identifiable individuals in a manner that could constitute surveillance, harassment, or a breach of their privacy.

Section 05

Third-party services — Anthropic API

TrustSmart AI uses the Anthropic Claude API to perform AI-powered content analysis. When you submit content for a check, that content is sent to Anthropic's servers for processing.

Anthropic is a US-based AI safety company. By using TrustSmart AI, you acknowledge that your submitted content will be processed by Anthropic's systems. Anthropic's use of data submitted via API is governed by their own privacy policy and terms of service, available at anthropic.com/legal/privacy.

As of the date of this policy, Anthropic does not use API-submitted content to train their models by default. We recommend reviewing Anthropic's current policy for the most up-to-date information.

We also use the following third-party services:

  • Netlify — hosting and serverless functions (netlify.com). Your requests pass through Netlify's infrastructure.
  • Google Analytics (GA4) — anonymised usage analytics. IP addresses are anonymised. You can opt out via Google's opt-out tools.
Section 06

How we use your information

We use the information we collect only for the following purposes:

  • To provide the genuineness checking service you have requested
  • To monitor and improve the performance and reliability of TrustSmart AI
  • To understand aggregate usage patterns and improve the product
  • To respond to enquiries you send us directly
  • To comply with our legal obligations under Australian law

We do not use your submitted content to train AI models. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for marketing purposes.

Section 07

How we store and protect your data

Submitted content (text, URLs, images, screenshots) is not stored by SmartLife AU. It is processed transiently and discarded after each check.

Anonymised analytics data collected via Google Analytics is stored on Google's servers in accordance with Google's data processing terms.

Any emails you send to us are stored in our business email system and retained only as long as necessary to resolve your enquiry.

Our infrastructure uses HTTPS encryption for all data in transit. Our serverless functions run in Netlify's secure environment and your Anthropic API key is never exposed to the browser.

Section 08

Cookies and analytics

TrustSmart AI uses Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymised usage data. GA4 may set cookies in your browser to distinguish unique sessions.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or any third-party marketing trackers. The only cookies present on TrustSmart AI are those set by Google Analytics for anonymised session tracking.

You can disable Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on, or by using a browser with tracking protection enabled.

Section 09

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Anthropic — submitted content is sent to Anthropic's API as described in Section 5
  • Netlify — requests are processed through Netlify's hosting infrastructure
  • Google Analytics — anonymised usage data only
  • Legal obligations — if required by Australian law, court order, or government authority

No other sharing occurs. We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or any other commercial third parties.

Section 10

Your rights under Australian Privacy Law

Under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to:

  • Request access to personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of personal information that is inaccurate, out of date, or incomplete
  • Make a complaint if you believe we have mishandled your personal information

Because TrustSmart AI does not store submitted content or require account creation, in most cases we will hold no personal information about you beyond any direct email correspondence.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@smartlifeau.com.au. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.
